📋 Introduction

Welcome to Neon Flow ("the Game"), developed by Wandering Penguin Studio's ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data when you play our Game on Android devices.

By downloading and playing Neon Flow, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

📦 Information We Collect

Neon Flow collects minimal data. Here is a breakdown:

  • Game progress data — your score, level reached, coins earned, and unlocked skins are saved locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to our servers.
  • Advertising data — our third-party advertising partner Unity Ads may collect device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), IP address, device model, OS version, and general location for the purpose of serving relevant advertisements. See the Third-Party Services section below.
  • No personal information — we do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

🎯 How We Use Information

  • To save your game progress locally so you can continue where you left off.
  • To display rewarded video advertisements that allow you to earn in-game coins or revive your character (via Unity Ads).
  • To improve the gameplay experience and fix bugs in future updates.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

🔒 Data Storage & Security

All game progress data is stored exclusively on your local device. We do not operate any servers that store your personal information. We implement reasonable measures to protect data within the app, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Advertising-related data collected by Unity Ads is governed by Unity's own privacy policy and security practices.

👶 Children's Privacy

Neon Flow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

The Game is rated for users aged 13 and above in accordance with Google Play content guidelines.

📣 Advertising & Your Choices

Neon Flow displays rewarded video ads powered by Unity Ads. These are optional — you only watch them to earn in-game coins or to revive your character. You are never forced to watch an ad.

To limit personalized advertising on your Android device:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads on your Android device.
  • Tap "Delete advertising ID" or enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization".
  • Note: opting out does not remove ads from the game; it only makes them less personalized.
